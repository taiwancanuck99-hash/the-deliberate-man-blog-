**Live deliberately. Choose meaning. Remain curious.**

---

## MORNING

- [ ] Wake at the same time
- [ ] No snooze
- [ ] Drink water / get light / breathe deeply
- [ ] Ask: *What kind of man will I be today?*
- [ ] Choose 3 words for the day: __________ / __________ / __________
- [ ] Remember: *Time is finite. This day matters.*
- [ ] 30+ minutes physical movement
- [ ] Begin with the most important task

---

## MIDDAY RESET

- [ ] Pause for 5 slow breaths
- [ ] Ask: *Am I deliberate or reactive?*
- [ ] Recommit to today's priority
- [ ] Encourage or contact one good person

---

## EVENING

- [ ] Walk / decompress without phone
- [ ] Journal:
  - What did I do well?
  - Where did I drift?
  - What must improve tomorrow?

---
*Print this out. Keep it visible. Do not drift.*
